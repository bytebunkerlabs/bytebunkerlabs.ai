  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

3.Legal bases

Where the GDPR or similar laws apply, we process personal data on the bases of your consent, our legitimate interests in operating and promoting our business, the performance of a contract with you, and compliance with legal obligations.

4.Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with trusted service providers who help us run the Services (for example, email, hosting, and analytics providers), under contracts that require them to protect it, and where required by law or to protect our rights.
